\subsection{Detailed Description}
Events that can be emitted, watched, and responded to with event handlers. 

Events are constructed with a jsonable value, as in 
\begin{DoxyCode}
Event(3.14);
Event(\textcolor{stringliteral}{"hello world"});
Event(\{1,2,3\});
\end{DoxyCode}
